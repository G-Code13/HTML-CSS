Welcome to Finnell Tennis Academy

Finnell Tennis Academy is a private lesson institution that was created by Robert Earl Finnell III. Coach Finnell focuses on teaching the techniques of the sport, along with training, match-play and fun games! It was created around 2017 and has been a huge success. Coach Finnell has coached for the CSAS girls team (2021) and lead them to win the State Championship. He has also been coaching at OLPH middle school for four years. Coach Finnell was voted Chattanooga Times 2021 high school tennis coach of the year (2021) and his daughter, Sunshine Finnell, was the player of the year. Together they have helped many students become successful in the sport.

Those Included

  • Robert Earl Finnell III (Head Coach)
  • Sunshine Finnell (Assistant Coach)
  • Jerrold J White II (Assistant Coach)

Coach Finnell's Philosophy

Coach Finnell's philosophy is to teach our students knowledge that will allow them to understand the sport as well as life skills. The goal is helping our students to be able to know what to do in matches in order for them to play their best with less stress.
