PC gaming

PCs have different advantages, including the ability to customize your PC, create hotkeys, sharper and more lifelike visuals, and the ability to modify games. There are three major components that PC gamers typically invest and upgrade. These componentes consist of the GPU (graphics processing Unit), RAM(memory), and Solid State Drives (SSD). While a lot of PC gamers think about the cost of building a PC, one of the lesser discussed advantages of PC gaming is the cost of PC games. With many digital distribution platforms offering PC games, this offers gamers an option to shop around and many find that PC games are cheaper than their console-specific versions.
